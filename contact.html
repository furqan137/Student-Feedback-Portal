Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I submit feedback on FeedbackHub?

+

Simply navigate to our Feedback Portal, fill out the form with your course details, instructor information, rating, and comments. Your feedback will be saved and can be managed from your dashboard.

Can I edit or delete my feedback after submission?

+

Yes! FeedbackHub allows you to edit or delete your feedback at any time. Simply go to the "Manage Feedback" section, find your submission, and use the edit or delete options.

What is the favorites feature?

+

The favorites feature allows you to mark important feedback submissions for easy access. You can add feedback to favorites by clicking the heart icon and view all your favorites from the favorites modal.

Is my feedback anonymous?

+

Your feedback is stored securely and only shared with authorized personnel. We offer options for anonymous feedback submission and respect your privacy preferences.

How do I contact technical support?

+

You can reach our technical support team via email at support@feedbackhub.edu, call us at (555) 123-4567, or use the live chat feature available on our website.
